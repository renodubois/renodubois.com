Bi-State Public Meeting Notes

Bi-State Development is the primariy entity in charge of transit in the St. Louis Metropolitan Area, in Missouri & Illinois. These notes are here to help increase transparency and raise awareness of topics covered at meetings.

Bi-State maintains a list of upcoming meetings, as well as past meetings’minutes here. You can view the meetings live on the Bi-State Facebook page, and you can watch past meetings on the Bi-State YouTube channel.

Want to express your opinion in one of these meetings? Bi-State has an online form for public comment available here.

If you have any questions about the notes taken here, or if you notice a typo, please let me know via email or mention me on Mastodon.
